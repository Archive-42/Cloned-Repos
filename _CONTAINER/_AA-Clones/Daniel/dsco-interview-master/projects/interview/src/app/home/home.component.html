Is this my interview?

Yes it is! In-person interviews can be stressful, lead to snap judgements, and often fail to show your competence at coding.

Who the heck knows the space complexity of the radix sort algorithm off the top of their head anyway???

Here at Dsco, we are trying something a little different.

Why should I take the time to do this interview?

Why should I take the time to do this interview?
  • Dsco is absolutely killing it in the retail supply chain marketplace.
  • You'll get to work with amazing customers - Nordstrom, Nike, Luxottica, Neiman Marcus, Bass Pro Shops, and Kohl's, just to name a few!
  • You will be exposed to the latest tech patterns and approaches: big data computation in the cloud, serverless development, varied databases (relational, document, hybrid, etc.), and more!
  • Dsco has a very relaxed policy around working from home and out-of-state work. We also have a great office stocked with food, treats, and some of the best coworkers you can find.
What is my quest?

You seek to add a new Authors feature to sit aside the existing Books feature.

What will my quest require?
  • A new lazy-loaded AuthorsModule to contain the Authors feature.
  • A new AuthorsComponent page that allows you to search for authors, displaying both their name & age.
  • A new ngrx Store to maintain the state for the feature.
  • A new AuthorsMocks class to mock out the AuthorSearchRequest.
Where should I begin?
  • First, play around with the Books feature using the navbar on the left. Note: It's generated data, so the search is pretty terrible. Try short words like "the", "he", "she".
  • To see how the books search request is mocked, go here: projects/interview/src/app/mock/books.mocks.ts
  • The ngrx Store for the Books feature is here: projects/interview/src/app/books/store
What if I wish to reach the Holy Grail?

Although the main quest is treacherous, an experienced Angular/ngrx developer should be able to complete it in about 30 minutes.

After this, to truly prove worthy of the Holy Grail, you may choose to embark on some side quests:

  • Dazzle the realm at the Knights Tourney with your html/css prowess!
  • Protect the villagers by conquering the evil TODOs.
  • Build a bridge between Authors and Books, allowing viewing books for specific authors and vice-versa.
  • Win the hearts of the kingdom by showing your character! Use the Holy Hand Grenade to randomly make the page blow up, leave a funny comment, brush off a few flesh wounds, and have fun!
